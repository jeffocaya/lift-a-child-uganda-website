Our Vision & Mission

Our Vision

A society where every child, regardless of ability or circumstance, is given a chance to have a dream and be empowered to live with dignity, opportunity, and joy.

Our Mission

To support and uplift vulnerable children and children born with disabilities through inclusive care, education, advocacy, and community engagement fostering environments where every child can thrive, belong, and reach their full potential.

About Us

Learn about our journey, values, and the team behind Lift A Child Uganda

Our Story

Lift A Child Uganda was founded in 2010 with a simple yet powerful vision: to create a world where every child, regardless of ability or circumstance, is given a chance to have a dream and be empowered to live with dignity, opportunity, and joy.

What began as a small community initiative has grown into a recognized force for change in Uganda's disability sector. Our journey started when our founder, Isaac Opoka, witnessed the profound challenges faced by children with disabilities and their families in rural communities.

Our Values

Compassion

We lead with empathy and kindness in all our interactions, recognizing the inherent dignity of every person we serve.

Inclusion

We believe in a world where every child belongs, regardless of ability, and work to create communities that embrace diversity.

Advocacy

We courageously speak up for the rights of children with disabilities, challenging stigma and discrimination wherever it exists.

Community

We believe in the power of community-driven solutions and work alongside families to create sustainable change.

Our Core Initiatives

Community Based Rehabilitation

We provide home-based rehabilitation services to help children with disabilities develop functional skills and maximize their potential.

  • Physical therapy sessions
  • Occupational therapy
  • Assistive device provision

Education Support

We ensure children with disabilities have access to quality education and learning resources.

  • School fee assistance
  • Learning materials provision
  • Teacher training programs

Nutrition & Basic Needs

We address malnutrition and provide essential resources to ensure children with disabilities can thrive.

  • Supplemental feeding programs
  • Nutritional education
  • Clothing and footwear

Family Counseling

We provide psychosocial support to families raising children with disabilities.

  • Individual counseling sessions
  • Support groups
  • Parenting workshops

Advocacy & Rights Protection

We work to protect the rights of children with disabilities and promote inclusive policies.

  • Awareness campaigns
  • Policy advocacy
  • Legal support
